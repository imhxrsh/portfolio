Our Services

Transport Services

In today's globalized economy, specialized shipping services play a crucial role in facilitating the transportation of various goods across borders. One such industry that has witnessed significant growth is the specialized shipping sector, particularly focusing on Purified Terephthalic Acid (PTA), Polypropylene Homopolymer (PP Bags), Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET Chips), Coil Trailers, TMT bar, Cement, Import Containers from Mundra, Pipavav, Nhava Sheva port, and expertise in handling Break Bulk cargo from Hazira and Mumbai port. This essay aims to shed light on the importance of specialized shipping services in enhancing global commerce and the significance of the mentioned services in the context of international trade.

  • Purified Terephthalic Acid (PTA):Specialized shipping services for PTA are essential due to its extensive application in the production of polyester fibers, resins, and films. Efficient transportation of PTA ensures a stable supply chain for industries dependent on this critical raw material.

  • Polypropylene Homopolymer (PP Bags):The transportation of PP bags demands specialized shipping solutions to ensure their safe and timely delivery. PP bags are used extensively in various industries for packaging, making efficient shipping crucial for the smooth functioning of businesses.

  • Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET Chips):PET chips, widely used in the production of plastic bottles and packaging materials, require specialized shipping services for efficient and secure transport. Smooth transportation of PET chips supports the packaging industry, ensuring the timely availability of raw materials.

  • Coil Trailers, TMT bar, Cement:Specialized shipping services for coil trailers, TMT bars, and cement are vital for the construction and infrastructure sectors. Safe and timely transportation of these materials ensures seamless project execution and contributes to the growth of the construction industry.

  • Import Containers from Mundra, Pipavav, Nhava Sheva Port:Specialized shipping services that focus on importing containers from major ports like Mundra, Pipavav, and Nhava Sheva are critical for a well-established trade network. Efficient handling and transportation of containers from these ports facilitate international trade, contributing to economic development.

  • Expertise in Handling Break Bulk Cargo from Hazira and Mumbai Port:Break bulk cargo handling expertise in ports like Hazira and Mumbai is crucial for the efficient transportation of large, non-containerized goods. Specialized shipping services in this domain ensure the safe and secure handling of diverse cargo, promoting trade activities.

FTL Transportation

JRS' FTL offers easy and affordable trucking solutions backed by extensive industry experience for all customers. You can count on us for all your high volume and high capacity needs in a cost- and time-efficient manner through our Full-Truckload (FTL) logistics.

FTL logistics from JRS combine specialised equipment and efficient delivery services to handle your heavier loads. You can enjoy a number of benefits by using JRS's FTL services, with expert professionals on hand to help you all the way. Among them are:
  • Safe Delivery : If you ship your cargo FTL, it will be handled in significantly less time. Full truckload shipping eliminates the need to break down, consolidate, or deconsolidate your cargo. As a result, there is less chance of your freight being damaged, and insurance claims are easier to deal with.
  • High-Speed Delivery : FTL shipping means your freight stays on one truck for the duration of the trip. Thus the truck driver’s primary and only concern is to transport your products to you. This helps us streamline. Streamlines your company's transportation and can greatly speed up the process by removing all the extra steps associated with Less-Than-Truckload shipping.
  • Suitable for a variety of shipments : FTL is the most convenient and arguably the best way to ship items of more than 4500 kgs or 12 to 15 cubic meters. Fragile products or products deemed high risk, as well as hazardous, can be effectively transported by FTL.
  • Zero Pilferages : JRS guarantees Zero shortages, no thefts, and no damage. Our goal is to deliver the client's cargo in its original condition.


We have the Best Trucking Solutions-
  • Real-Time Fleet Monitoring : With real-time asset tracking and monitoring of our vehicles, we reduce downtime and increase productivity.
  • Electronic Proof of Delivery: A major perk of moving logistics towards digitized means is electronic proof of delivery. We are totally paperless now. This not only reduces our costs but also simplifies the entire process for fleet managers and drivers a like.. By using electronic means, data entry is automatically generated and requires less time and energy than manual data entry. Moreover, it provides drivers with all necessary and relevant information, making their job easier.
  • Contactless Delivery: To minimize or eliminate physical contact and comply with the rules of social distancing, we have started completely cashless deliveries. Now you can pay online with your Digi-wallets.

Fleet Management

Fleet is an integral part of every logistics company. At JRS Fleet Management, we prioritize vehicle maintenance, driver satisfaction, cost control, increased productivity, and compliance to deliver customer satisfaction.

How Does JRS Fleet Management Maintain its Quality Standards?

  • Our Driver Counseling Supervisors educate our drivers on Road Safety and Defense training for every trip.
  • In our counseling centers, we also monitor the behavior of our Drivers and correct them if we see any problem. This way, we reduce the probability of accidents.
  • As part of a special agreement, we have teamed up with Tata Motors/Ashok Leyland to maintain our vehicles in their high-end workshops. This helps us to ensure the quality-life of our fleet and reduce breakdowns, delays, and accidents.


Impact of our Fleet Management on Customers

  • The vehicle runs for High uptime and takes lower mean time to repair
  • Lesser breakdowns due to better preventive maintenance
  • Improved mean time between failures
  • Improved Vehicle Life cycle means faster delivery with lower transit time.
  • Safe and Secure end-to-end deliveries without any delays or accidents.
